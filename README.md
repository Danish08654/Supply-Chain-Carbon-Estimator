An ML-powered system that automates Scope 3 emission estimation, supplier carbon risk scoring, and ESG reporting turning weeks of manual analysis into seconds.

---

##  The Problem

Scope 3 emissions  indirect emissions across the supply chain account for **70–90% of a company’s total carbon footprint**.

With increasing regulatory pressure, companies are now required to report these emissions.

However, most organizations still rely on:

* Manual spreadsheets
* Static emission factors
* Time-consuming reporting cycles (weeks per report)
* Outputs that lack audit reliability

---

##  What It Does

###  Emission Estimation

* Estimates **kg CO₂e per procurement item**
* Uses **GHG Protocol-aligned emission factors**
* Handles large-scale supplier datasets

---

###  Carbon Risk Scoring

* Scores each supplier on a **0–100 carbon risk scale**
* Considers emission intensity, spend, and activity patterns

---

### 🏷 ESG Tier Assignment

Suppliers are categorized into:

* 🟢 **Green** → Low carbon risk
* 🟡 **Yellow** → Moderate risk
* 🔴 **Red** → High risk

---

###  Reduction Insights

* Identifies **top emission reduction opportunities**
* Provides **estimated impact (%) per action**

---

###  Transport Impact Analysis

* Compares carbon footprint across **transport modes**
* Enables smarter logistics decisions

---

###  Automated ESG Reporting

* Generates **audit-ready PDF reports**
* Built using **ReportLab**
* Includes emissions, risk scores, and ESG tiers

---

###  API + Dashboard

* **FastAPI REST API** for real-time access
* **Streamlit dashboard** for interactive visualization

---

##  Tech Stack

* **Machine Learning:** Python, LightGBM / Scikit-learn
* **Backend:** FastAPI
* **Data Processing:** Pandas, NumPy
* **Visualization:** Streamlit, Plotly
* **Reporting:** ReportLab (PDF generation)

---

##  Key Impact

This system enables organizations to:

* Reduce Scope 3 reporting time from **weeks → seconds**
* Improve **audit readiness and compliance**
* Identify **high-risk suppliers instantly**
* Make **data-driven sustainability decisions**
